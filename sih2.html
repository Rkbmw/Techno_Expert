About TempleFlow

Our Mission

TempleFlow was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to transform the spiritual experience for pilgrims by leveraging technology to manage crowd flow and reduce wait times.

We understand that visiting temples and holy sites should be a peaceful, contemplative experience, not a stressful one spent in long queues.

Our team of engineers, data scientists, and cultural heritage experts work together to create solutions that respect tradition while embracing innovation.

Our Approach

We use a combination of IoT sensors, computer vision, and predictive analytics to monitor crowd density in real-time and provide actionable insights to temple administrators.

Our booking system helps distribute visitors throughout the day, preventing overcrowding and ensuring everyone has a meaningful experience.

Our Values

Respect for Tradition

We work closely with religious authorities to ensure our solutions enhance rather than disrupt sacred practices.

Privacy & Security

We prioritize the privacy of pilgrims and ensure all data is handled with the utmost care and security.

Sustainability

Our solutions help reduce the environmental impact of overcrowding at heritage sites.

Book Your Visit

Plan Your Spiritual Journey

Booking your visit in advance helps us manage crowd flow and ensures you have a peaceful experience at the temple.

Select your preferred date and time slot based on availability. You'll receive a confirmation with a QR code for entry.

Same-day bookings are available but subject to current crowd conditions.

Digital Pass

Your booking confirmation includes a QR code that serves as your entry pass. Show it at the gate for quick entry.

Visitor Guidelines

Arrive on Time

Please arrive within your selected time slot to help maintain crowd flow and avoid congestion.

Follow Safety Protocols

Wear masks in crowded areas and maintain appropriate physical distance where possible.

Respect Sacred Spaces

Remove footwear where required and maintain silence in meditation and prayer areas.

Digital Tickets

Keep your booking confirmation QR code ready for scanning at the entrance.

Facility Usage

Use restrooms and other facilities during non-peak hours to avoid crowding.

Photography

Respect signage regarding photography restrictions. Avoid blocking pathways while taking photos.

Appropriate Attire

Dress modestly as per temple traditions. Some temples may provide coverings if needed.

Donations

Use designated donation boxes. Avoid handing money directly to priests or staff.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance can I book my temple visit?

You can book your temple visit up to 30 days in advance. Same-day bookings are also available depending on current crowd conditions.

What if I'm running late for my time slot?

We understand that delays can happen. If you're running late, you'll still be allowed entry, but you may experience longer wait times depending on the current crowd situation.

Is the booking fee refundable?

Booking fees are generally non-refundable. However, in exceptional circumstances such as temple closures or emergencies, refunds will be processed automatically.

Can I modify my booking after confirmation?

Yes, you can modify your booking up to 24 hours before your scheduled visit through the "Manage Booking" section on our website.

Are there any discounts for senior citizens or children?

Yes, senior citizens (above 65 years) and children below 5 years enjoy free entry. Children between 5-12 years receive a 50% discount on the booking fee.

What measures are in place for visitors with disabilities?

We provide wheelchair accessibility, priority entry, and dedicated assistance for visitors with disabilities. Please mention your requirements during booking.

How accurate is the crowd status information?

Our crowd status is updated in real-time using advanced sensors and AI algorithms. The information is typically 95% accurate, but sudden changes in visitor flow can occur.
